FAQ

Important platform boundaries

Does ApplyPilot auto-apply to every job?

No. Direct submission happens only through approved employer or ATS integrations, after candidate review and consent.

What happens for unsupported jobs?

The platform generates job-specific documents and answers, then the Chrome extension helps autofill the external form. The candidate reviews and submits manually.

Can AI invent experience to improve my chances?

No. AI can rewrite truthful bullet points, emphasize relevant skills, and reorder sections. It must not invent employers, degrees, projects, certifications, or years of experience.

Do free candidates get direct apply?

Free candidates can search jobs, save a limited number, and open official apply links. Direct apply and assisted apply are paid-plan workflows.
